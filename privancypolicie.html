Privacy Policy

MONQUOTIDIEN RENT CAR

This app lets you view car rental information and start a message to us (email or WhatsApp). No account, no in-app payments, and no personal or sensitive user data is collected by the app.

1) Data Collection

The app itself does not collect, store, share, or sell personal or sensitive user data. If you choose to contact us by email or WhatsApp from the app, we receive the information you include in your message (e.g., name, dates, car preference) only so we can respond to your inquiry.

2) Data Sharing

The app does not share user data with third parties. Messages you send by email or WhatsApp are handled by those services and by our mailbox/WhatsApp account. We do not add your details to marketing lists.

3) Permissions

The app does not request access to location, contacts, camera, microphone, photos, or storage.

4) Booking / Contact

When you tap “Contact” or submit the booking form, your device opens your email app addressed to: rent@monquotidien.ink (CC: support@monquotidien.ink), or opens WhatsApp to chat with us. The app does not keep a copy of your message; it exists in your email/WhatsApp outbox and in our inbox.

5) Third-Party Services

The app does not include analytics SDKs, ads, or trackers. If you use email or WhatsApp, your use of those services is governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

6) Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7) Your Choices

  • No account is required; nothing to delete inside the app.
  • To remove messages you sent us, you can delete them from your email/WhatsApp and request deletion from our records at any time.
